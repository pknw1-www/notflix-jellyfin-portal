How To Install Jellyfin
Method #1: Enable unknown sources

Step 1. Open your Firestick device and, using the remote, scroll to the settings section and click “MY Fire TV.”
Step 2. Navigate to the “developer tab” click “apps from unknown sources.”
Step 3. The Firestick device will request you to confirm the action.

Press the “turn on” button to continue.

Method 2: Download the Jellyfin app

Step 1. Download the jellyfin app on the Firestick.    
Step 2. Get a downloader app from your web browser or app store.

Step 3. Now, click on download now button to get the downloader app and then select open to launch.

Step 4. Next, choose the ‘Allow’ button and then click on the URL field and enter Jellyfin download URL: https://www.amazon.com/gp/aw/d/B07TX7Z725 and click the “GO ” button.

Step 5. Click download to complete the process.

The process is complete, and you can enjoy the streaming services - start the App and use server https://jellyfin.pknw1.co.uk
